# Forecasting Petty Crimes During Major Holidays in the United States

## Overview
Petty crime outlier detection at the city and state level using several different time series models to determine if crime levels showed a statistical increase in the time surrounding major holidays in the United States.

## Problem
Law enforcement agencies need to plan their resource scheduling around the holidays and need to make sure they have the appropriate resources necessary to handle expected crime levels around the holidays.

## Approach
ARIMA/SARIMA XGBoost, and CUSUM were used for anomaly detection to determine if the holiday periods contained outlier crime levels that would require additional law enforcement resources.

## Results
Against initial expectations, crime levels for the time periods surrounding the holidays didn't show a consistent, significant increase compared to the rest of the year.
